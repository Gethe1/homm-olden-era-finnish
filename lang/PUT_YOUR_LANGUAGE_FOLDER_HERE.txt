Create one folder here named after your language (e.g. "finnish"), containing:
  - displayname.txt   (one line: what shows in the in-game dropdown, e.g. "Suomi")
  - texts/*.json       (your translated files, same names/format as the game's own)

See README.md for the full format and a worked example.
This file itself is harmless to leave here -- only subfolders are scanned.
